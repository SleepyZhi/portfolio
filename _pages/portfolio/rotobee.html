rotobee

Rotobee was a small studio in Berlin with 2 released games at the time I've joined. I joined to write my thesis about an online multiplayer framework for their engine and parralel I also supported the development of their current game. Rotobee was the reason for me to move to Berlin and overall it was a great learning experience with a great team. Unfortunately Rotobee wen't bancrupt and did not release any game after Kiss Before Midnight.
