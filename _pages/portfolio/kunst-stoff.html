kunst-stoff

Kunst-Stoff was a independent agency and game studio and for me a quite special one because working there always felt more like working with friends instead of just co-workers. Also kunst-stoff always had a quite high artistic expectations for all its projects no matter if it was own projects or works for customers. As Development Director I was heading the development of several projects and many of them also have been built with the orkitec game engine.
